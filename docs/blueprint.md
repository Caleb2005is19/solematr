# **App Name**: SoleMate

## Core Features:

- Shoe Browsing: Browse shoes by brand, style, and size with filtering options.
- Product Details: View detailed product pages with high-quality images, descriptions, and customer reviews. 
- AI Powered Style Recommendation: Provide AI-driven style recommendations based on user preferences and browsing history; the AI tool decides when a specific style of shoe should be displayed.
- Shopping Cart Management: Manage a shopping cart with options to add, remove, and modify quantities.
- Secure Checkout: Complete a secure checkout process with various payment options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Off-white (#F2F2F2) to create a clean, spacious feel.
- Background color: Dark gray (#222222) for contrast and to highlight the shoes.
- Accent color: Subtle gold (#D4AF37) for highlights and call-to-action elements, providing a touch of luxury.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, providing a clean, modern and easily readable experience.
- Minimalist icons for navigation and key actions, using a thin stroke style.
- Clean and spacious layout with a focus on showcasing the shoes; use of white space to avoid clutter.
- Subtle animations and transitions on product pages and during the checkout process to enhance user engagement.
